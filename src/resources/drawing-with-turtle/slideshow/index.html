Warm up: Spirograph

  • Work with your table to draw a spiral using the spirograph.
  • Discuss: How often does the spiral repeat?
  • Brainstorm instructions so another table can draw your spiral.
  • Exchange instructions with the table to your left. Were your instructions effective? How could you make your instructions easier to understand?

Guided practice: Setting up Wipple

  • You can make a computer draw your spiral using similar instructions!
  • Work with a partner
  • Open your computers and go to wipple.org

Turn and talk: The repeatrepeat command


Read your instructions for drawing a square. How many instructions do you have? How could you use fewer instructions?

Listen for: repeat, times, again

The repeatrepeat command



{ means begin repeat

} means end repeat
